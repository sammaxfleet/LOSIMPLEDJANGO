About this space

The house is intended to bring together local Mediterranean architecture and minimal contemporary design. The L-shape of the house is rendered white and minimal finishes are used through the interior. Downstairs, the open plan design is connected by a terrace, giving the space an indoor-outdoor feel. Upstairs, 2 bedrooms and a bathroom open onto a private terrace which looks out towards the beach. Lo Simple translates to “Be Simple” in English. The minimalistic aesthetic is designed for you to simply relax,rejuvenate & be in peace. It is located in quiet surroundings in the beautiful city of Barcelona. We describe it as a quiet escape.
